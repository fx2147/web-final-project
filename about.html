About Me

I’m a college student who has grown through tutoring, dance, and community leadership. Teaching and performing help me connect with people, while my volunteer work has taught me how to organize projects and support others.

Jump to Services

Learning, movement, and community

I started tutoring classmates in high school and quickly realized how much I enjoy explaining concepts in a way that makes sense. Dance has been part of my life for many years as well. Performing and teaching choreography have given me confidence in front of groups and helped me learn how to communicate nonverbally.

Today, I bring together academic skills, creativity, and leadership experience to support students in different spaces: the classroom, the studio, and community events.

Leadership & Community Impact

During high school, I founded the Great Neck chapter of AYLUS (Alliance of Youth Leaders in the United States), a national volunteer organization that encourages youth to lead community service and educational initiatives.

My name is listed under the official founding members on the organization’s website: AYLUS Great Neck Chapter .
